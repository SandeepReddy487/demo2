M/s Tejaswini Industries is one of the pioneers engaged in manufacturing and supplying of a huge assortment of Transformers and all types of Electrical Panels. The wide range of products and services offered by us includes Industrial Transformers and Transformer Repair Services and Special purpose transformers. Also, our company is highly instrumental in offering reliable and efficient services for repairing transformers and their oil filtration. The wide range of products available with us is designed and developed in line with the quality standards set by the industry. We consider the ever-increasing demands of different industries and offer innovative range of products. The products offered by us like PLC Control Panel, APFC Control Panel, Distribution Control Panel, Power Factor Correction Panel, Electric Switchgear Panels, Unitized Substation, Dry Type Transformers, Electrical Power Transformer and many more are well appreciated in industry. We fabricate the high performing transformers by making use of quality spare parts and components that ensure their power efficiency and longer service life


We firmly believe in quality and that too backed up by after-sales services to their installations throughout INDIA. We are based at a growing industrial zone of the country. The city is well connected by air, rail, and road. We are manufacturing ISI & BIS approved onload distribution transformers starting from 100 KVA to 2500 KVA under BIS and above 2500 KVA up-to 5000 KVA under IS-2026 of all voltage class and covering all level of efficiency and Power transformer ranges from 2 MVA to 5 MVA of 33 kV voltage class.


Tejaswini industries an ISO 9001:2015 certified company was established in 2010 in one of the prime Industrial Estate of Hyderabad. Later on the company established another facility in one industrial estates nearby Siricilla, Telangana, where the sick(repair) transformers are repaired. We are one of the reliable transformer manufacturers in the country that have been serving the industrial establishments and other end users since 2010. The transformers manufactured by Tejaswini Industries are of superior quality and are widely used in different sectors.
